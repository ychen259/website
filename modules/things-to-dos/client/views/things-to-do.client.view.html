Explore Florida's Orchid City Stay Close to Fun Things To Do In West Palm Beach

From outlet shopping and history museums to world class performing arts, West Palm Beach offers visitors so many ways to relax and have fun. Take the kids to see exotic animals at the the Palm Beach Zoo or go on an outback adventure at the Lion Country Safari.
 
Wander through lush tropical landscapes at the Mounts Botanical Gardens or immerse yourself in Gilded Age glamour and history at the Flagler Museum. Shop for upscale bargains at the Palm Beach Outlets or enjoy shopping, dining and entertainment at the Rosemary Square.
 
Take in an MLB Spring Training baseball game at Ballpark of the Palm Beaches or try your luck with dog racing and table games at the Palm Beach Kennel Club. Whether you crave arts and entertainment, thrilling race action or family fun, get out to discover some of the best things to do near our hotel in West Palm Beach.

Los Angeles County Arboretum

The arboretum's plants are grouped by geography with gardens for South American, Mediterranean, South African, Australian and Asiatic-North American plants. Other displays include the Aquatic Garden, Meadowbrook, Demonstration Home Gardens, Garden for All Seasons, Prehistoric and Jungle Garden, Native Oaks, Herb Garden, and the Palm and Bamboo collection.
