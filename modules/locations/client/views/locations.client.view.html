Explore Florida's Orchid City From our West Palm Beach Hotel Near The Airport

Situated just two miles from the Palm Beach International Airport, Holiday Inn Palm Beach Airport offers easy access to top business destinations, exciting attractions and some of Florida's best nightlife. Enjoy shopping and dining at the Rosemary Square or bag some deals at the Palm Beach Outlets.
 
See world-class performers and touring Broadway shows at the Kravis Center. Travel back to the Gilded Age with a tour of the Flagler Museum or catch an MLB spring training game at Roger Dean Stadium.
 
Take the kids on a drive-through safari adventure or go meet the animals at the Palm Beach Zoo. Holiday Inn Palm Beach Airport places you in the center of the action so you can get out and experience the best of West Palm Beach.
