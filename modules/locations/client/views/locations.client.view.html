Explore Los Angeles From our Aztec Hotel

Situated just 0.5 miles from the Old Town Monrovia, Aztec Hotel offers easy access to exciting attractions and some of Los Angeles's best nightlife. Enjoy shopping and dining at the Santa Anita Mall.
 
Go hiking and embrace nature at Hermit Fall and Big Santa Anita Canyon. Travel to 16 different type of gardens with a tour of the Art Museum or have an relaxing experience at Los Angeles County Arboretum.
 
Take the kids go meet Mickey at the Disneyland Park or enjoy the sunshine on Seal Beach and Long Beach. Aztec Hotel places you in the center of the action so you can get out and experience the best of Los Angeles.
