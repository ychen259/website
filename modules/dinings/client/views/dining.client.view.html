Savor Coastal Florida Cuisine At our Restaurant and Bar in West Palm Beach

Coastal Florida is a vibrant food and dining destination, where fresh local seafood and exotic international cooking traditions come together in a melting pot of flavors. Start your West Palm Beach culinary adventures with creative American and international fare with all-day dining in the casual atmosphere of h street grille. Wake up to a healthy breakfast of all your morning favorites.
Take a lunch break or enjoy a relaxing dinner with dishes that include seafood, steaks, pastas, salads, burgers and flat breads. Sip cocktails, wines and craft beers while you enjoy appetizers and your favorite sports at the bar. We also offer in-room dining so you can enjoy relaxed dining in the comfort and privacy of your room.
